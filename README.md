What is Zentangle?

Zentangle is an application platform and decentralized ecosystem, the first app launching on our platform is Vision Legends, a gamified AI training system that connects Data Scientists, Gamers, and Artists. With Vision Legends we aim to decentralize and open the $0.5B Data annotation industry to anyone looking for high quality data, giving the data scientists and users true ownership of their datasets and disrupting large industry players like Google, Microsoft, Apple, and Facebook (to name a few).

What is Vision Legends

For Game Developers, Vision Legends represent an alternative revenue stream to replace addvertising allowing them to provide a more meaninful use of gamers attention by doing image annotations that will help with the training of Artificial Intelligence mondels.

For Data Scientist, Vision Legends lets you create data sets to train your image recognition models by providing high quality, human verified, data sets for a fraction of the cost traditional providers charge. All images within a data set are annotated by players who receive monetary rewards based on their accuracy. The more accurate an image is annotated vs. other players, the higher their reward.

For Gamers, Vision Legends is a gamified experience that complements the gaming with the greater purpose of beign part of a swarm intelligence that collectively trains artificial intelligence. Gamers are rewarded for their time and effort.
